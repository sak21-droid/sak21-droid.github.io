Bike-Share Analytics

Comprehensive analysis of urban bike usage patterns with predictive modeling and optimization insights.

Overview

Dataset, approach, and key findings

Problem Statement

Urban bike-sharing systems generate massive data. The goal was to analyze usage patterns and predict demand to improve efficiency and reduce downtime.

Methodology

  • Collected and cleaned bike-share trip data
  • Exploratory data analysis to identify trends and peak usage
  • Developed predictive models for demand forecasting
  • Recommended station rebalancing and bike allocation strategies

Results

Analysis provided actionable insights into peak usage times, high-demand locations, and seasonal patterns. Predictive models achieved 87% accuracy in forecasting daily bike demand.
