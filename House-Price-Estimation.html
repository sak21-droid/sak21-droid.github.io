House Price Estimation

Ensemble regression model for predicting housing prices with feature engineering & data integration.

Overview

Details about the dataset, approach, and results

Problem Statement

Housing markets fluctuate due to numerous factors. The goal was to predict house prices using a regression model trained on historical data.

Methodology

  • Data cleaning & preprocessing
  • Feature engineering (location, size, amenities, etc.)
  • Modeling with Linear Regression, Random Forests, XGBoost
  • Model stacking for improved accuracy

Results

The final ensemble achieved 92% R² score on the validation set, outperforming baseline models.
