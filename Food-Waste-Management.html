Food Waste Management System

Data-driven platform to optimize food usage, reduce waste, and promote sustainability.

Overview

Details about the problem, approach, and key outcomes

Problem Statement

Food wastage is a global issue causing environmental and economic concerns. The aim was to design a system that tracks, analyzes, and reduces food waste across households and businesses.

Methodology

  • Designed a database for tracking consumption and waste
  • Implemented machine learning models for predicting usage patterns
  • Developed a dashboard for monitoring and insights
  • Integrated alert system for surplus food donations

Results

The system successfully identified high-waste food categories, provided actionable recommendations, and improved food resource allocation efficiency.
Key outcome: Demonstrated a 15–20% reduction in waste in test cases.
