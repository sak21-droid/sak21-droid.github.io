SpaceX Falcon 9 Predictor

Machine learning pipeline predicting Falcon 9 rocket landing success using historical flight and weather data.

Overview

Approach, dataset, and key results

Problem Statement

Predicting the landing success of Falcon 9 rockets is complex due to multiple environmental and technical factors. The goal was to develop a predictive ML model to forecast successful landings.

Methodology

  • Collected historical launch and landing data
  • Feature engineering including weather, booster info, and mission type
  • Trained models: Logistic Regression, Random Forest, XGBoost
  • Evaluated using cross-validation and ensemble stacking for higher accuracy

Results

The final ensemble model achieved 89% accuracy in predicting successful landings. This provides actionable insights for mission planning and risk assessment.
