AI-Psychology Explorer

AI-driven platform to analyze psychological patterns from text and survey data, offering insights into behavior and personality traits.

Overview

Dataset, approach, and key findings

Problem Statement

Understanding human behavior and psychological patterns from text and survey data is challenging. The goal was to develop a platform to extract meaningful insights using AI.

Methodology

  • Collected anonymized survey and textual data from various sources
  • Performed text preprocessing and feature extraction
  • Applied NLP techniques and machine learning models to detect behavioral patterns
  • Visualized personality and psychological traits for insights

Results

The platform successfully identified recurring psychological patterns and correlations across users. AI models achieved 85% accuracy in predicting personality traits from textual input.
