Spatiotemporal Wave Analysis

Applied PCA/SVD techniques on coastal wave video data to extract dominant spatial and temporal patterns.

Overview

Methodology, dataset, and insights

Problem Statement

Monitoring coastal wave patterns is critical for environmental and engineering applications. The goal was to analyze wave dynamics through spatiotemporal video data.

Methodology

  • Collected high-resolution coastal wave videos
  • Applied preprocessing: standardization and noise filtering
  • Projected videos onto dominant color axis
  • Performed spatial PCA to extract dominant spatial patterns
  • Derived temporal principal components and reconstructed low-rank approximations

Results

Analysis revealed dominant wave motion patterns, temporal periodicity, and compression efficiency. Provided actionable insights for coastal monitoring and environmental studies.
